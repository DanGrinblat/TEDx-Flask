After training as a physicist and mathematician, Ton van den Bogert became a graduate student in the department of veterinary anatomy where his equestrian fascination began. Having studied everything from computer-aided movement analysis to Sports medicine, Professor van den Bogert’s unique insight into the mechanics of motion is revolutionizing robotics. He currently holds the Parker-Hannifin Endowed Chair in Human Motion and Control in the Department of Mechanical Engineering here at Cleveland State University.
